About Loita Hills

Loita Hills protects the sacred 'Forest of the Lost Child' - 33,000 hectares of pristine montane forest managed exclusively by the local Maasai community, making it one of Kenya's few ungazetted forests. Multi-day treks traverse rolling hills at 2,000-2,600m elevation through dense cedar forests sheltering 2,000+ colobus monkeys, forest elephants, leopards, and over 180 bird species.

The Entasopia Falls provide refreshing swimming opportunities, while panoramic viewpoints reveal Mount Kilimanjaro, Ol Donyo Lengai volcano, and Lake Magadi.
