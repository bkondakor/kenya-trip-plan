Booking & Logistics

Book NOW

January is peak season; Il Ngwesi and Saruni properties fill 12+ months ahead. Don't delay!

Domestic Flights

Use Safarilink or AirKenya; book with lodge packages for better coordination and peace of mind.

Luggage Requirements

Soft-sided luggage only with a 15kg limit on safari planes. Good news: laundry is available at all lodges, so you can pack lighter.

eTA (Electronic Travel Authorization)

Apply immediately at etakenya.go.ke. Cost: $32.50 and it requires 3+ days processing. Plan ahead!

Plan for $9,000-13,000 per person (mid-range) including domestic flights, accommodation, and most activities. This covers the high season pricing for January.

Park Fees Advantage

January has 50% lower Mara park fees vs. July-Oct. Though you're skipping Mara for hidden gems, you still benefit from lower costs during this period.

Health & Safety

Required Documentation

Yellow fever certificate is required if coming through endemic countries. Get vaccinated well before departure.

Malaria Prophylaxis

Recommended, especially for Samburu and Northern Kenya. Consult your travel doctor 6 weeks before departure to start medication early.

Regional Risk Levels

  • ‚Ä¢ Samburu/Northern Kenya: Malaria risk present
  • ‚Ä¢ Coast: Lower malaria risk

Water Safety

Drink bottled water only. However, lodges provide filtered water, which is safe for brushing teeth and general use.

Travel Insurance

Mandatory! Make sure coverage includes emergency evacuation for remote areas. You'll be visiting some isolated locations.

Weather Strategy

January Climate

Dry season overall, but pack one light rain jacket just in case. Rare afternoon showers are possible.

Samburu Heat

Expect hot temperatures at 32¬∞C+. Hydrate constantly, wear light-colored clothing, and use high SPF sunscreen.

Loita Hills Terrain

Can be muddy from December rains. Bring boots with good tread for secure footing on wet trails.

Coastal Perfection

The coast offers ideal conditions: 28¬∞C, low humidity, and calm seas. Pack lighter clothing here.

Dawn and dusk game drives are scheduled game drive times AND they offer perfect golden hour light. You get both adventure and stunning photography!

Cultural Photography Ethics

Ask permission before photographing people, especially at Il Ngwesi and Maji Moto. Respect builds relationships.

Maximizing Cultural Experiences

Basic Swahili Phrases

Learning these will delight locals and enrich your connections:

  • ‚Ä¢ "Jambo" - Hello (traditional greeting)
  • ‚Ä¢ "Asante" - Thank you
  • ‚Ä¢ "Karibu" - Welcome

Authentic Engagement

At Il Ngwesi and Maji Moto: engage genuinely, ask real questions, and skip performative photo ops. Authentic connections matter more than Instagram shots.

Direct Artisan Support

Purchase crafts directly from artisan cooperatives at lodges. You'll get better prices AND the money goes directly to the makers.

Conservation Impact Tours

Request school or clinic visits through lodge managers. Seeing the conservation impact firsthand adds profound meaning to your journey.

Alternative Adjustments

Skip Matthews Range (Budget Option)

If budget-tight: skipping Matthews Range saves 1 flight + one expensive lodge night. Instead, extend your Samburu time for deeper immersion.

Watamu Alternative for Coastal Focus

Add Watamu instead of Lamu if you want snorkeling/diving focus. Easier access, dedicated marine park, and turtle sanctuary on-site.

Hell's Gate for Less Hiking

Swap Loita for Hell's Gate if you're less hiking-inclined. It's an easier day trip from Nairobi with a cycling option through the gorge.
