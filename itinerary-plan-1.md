Jan 17 (Fri) - Night Arrival

Arrive Nairobi evening → Transfer to airport hotel (Eka Hotel or Boma Nairobi)
Rest up for safari start

Jan 18-20 (Sat-Mon) - Laikipia Plateau (3 nights)

Morning drive to Laikipia (4-5 hours scenic)
Stay: Il Ngwesi Eco-Lodge (community-owned) OR Lewa Safari Camp
Activities: Rhino tracking on foot, game drives, camel walks, Samburu cultural visits, horseback safari (if Lewa Wilderness)

Jan 21-23 (Tue-Thu) - Samburu/Sera (3 nights)

Drive north to Samburu (2.5 hours)
Stay: Saruni Rhino (black rhino tracking) OR Saruni Samburu
Activities: On-foot rhino tracking, Samburu Special Five viewing, Reteti Elephant Sanctuary, Singing Wells visit

Jan 24 (Fri) - Matthews Range (1 night)

Fly Samburu → Ngelai airstrip
Stay: Sarara Camp or Kitich Camp
Activity: Afternoon guided forest walk

Jan 25-26 (Sat-Sun) - Loita Hills (2 nights)

Fly back south → Drive to Loita Hills (3 hours from Nairobi)
Stay: Maji Moto Maasai Cultural Camp
Activities: Multi-day hiking through sacred forest, Maasai cultural immersion, Entasopia Falls

Jan 27-31 (Mon-Fri) - Lamu Coast (4 nights)

Fly Nairobi → Lamu (via Manda Airport)
Stay: Peponi Hotel (Shela Beach) OR The Majlis Resort (Manda Island)
Activities: Dhow sailing, Lamu Old Town UNESCO site, Shela Beach, snorkeling, Swahili cooking class
Optional day trip: Pate Island ruins or Kiwayu Island

Jan 31 - Departure

Fly Lamu → Nairobi (morning flight)
Connect to international departure
