About Nairobi

Nairobi, Kenya's bustling capital, serves as the perfect bookend to your adventure. While you won't spend extensive time here, the city offers comfortable airport hotels for your arrival and departure nights. The city's elevation at 1,795m means pleasant year-round temperatures around 25¬∞C during the day and 12¬∞C at night in January.

If you have extra time, Nairobi offers several world-class attractions including the Giraffe Centre, David Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage, and the Karen Blixen Museum. The city's restaurants showcase Kenya's diverse cuisine, from nyama choma (grilled meat) to innovative fusion dining.
