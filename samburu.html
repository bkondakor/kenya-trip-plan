About Samburu & Sera

Samburu National Reserve showcases species found nowhere else in Kenya: the 'Samburu Special Five' including Grevy's zebra, reticulated giraffe, gerenuk, Beisa oryx, and Somali ostrich. The most extraordinary experience lies in Sera Community Conservancy, where the Samburu community welcomed 10 black rhinos back in 2015; today 23 rhinos roam the sanctuary with 12 calves born since reintroduction and zero poaching incidents.

January's hot, dry conditions concentrate wildlife spectacularly at the Ewaso Nyiro River, providing exceptional viewing opportunities.
